DOTS Cafe - About us

DOTS Cafe was born in the classroom as part of coursework during 2020. A fusion of meme and pun to bring you a new coffee producer.

We aim to provide an ethically sourced, high quality product. Working with our partners overseas we aim to produce lasting partnerships with our fellow communities.

Our online store services customers from around the world. With safe procurement and reliable deliveries.

Our physical store is set in the rustic East Limerick County,Ballyhoura region. Our Cafe provides a child-friendly and tranquil environment.

In store we supply a selection of affordable treats for your pleasure. Our recipes will often be shared on our social media pages on Facebook and YouTube.
